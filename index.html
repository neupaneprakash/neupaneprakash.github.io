About

Prakash Neupane native to eastern NEPAL is an MS scholar, working under the supervision of Dr. Manas Thakur (Assistant Professor at IIT Bombay) at School of Computing and Electrical Engineering (SCEE), Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi. I am fortunate to have Dr. Manas Thakur as my advisor.

I graduated with a bachelor's degree (B.Sc.) in Computer Science and Information Technology from Godawari College, affiliated to Institute of Science and Technology (IOST) , Tribhuvan  University in 2018.

My current field of research consolidate program analysis, compiler, and programming languages. Currently, I am working to study the effect of call-graphs in the precision and scalability of android taint analysis using popular framework called Soot which involvs analysis, optimization, instrumentation, and visualization of Java and Android apps.
